# Ideal Customer Profile — FINDGOD

> Describes who FINDGOD's ideal customer is. Claude uses this to write better copy, choose better content topics, and stay aligned with the audience.

## Demographics

- **Age:** 16-30 (sweet spot: 18-26)
- **Gender:** Male (the brand also reaches women but speaks primarily to men)
- **Location:** US-first, then English-speaking countries (UK, Canada, Australia, Ireland, NZ), then global
- **Life stage:** Late high school through early career, single or new in relationship, often without strong father figures or strong faith community

## Background

He was raised culturally Christian but never knew Christ personally. OR he was never raised in faith but feels something is missing. He associates church with corny branding, fake smiles, soft preaching, or scandals. He's not actively against Christianity — he just hasn't seen a version of it that respects his intelligence or his masculinity.

He may also be a Christian who is falling away or feels his current church doesn't speak to the pressures of being a young man in 2026.

## Pain Points

- **Anxiety + overwhelm** from constant noise: news, social media, dopamine apps, financial stress, AI displacing jobs
- **Lack of brotherhood** — feels isolated even when surrounded by people
- **No father figure or weak male role models** — searching for what a real man looks like
- **Stuck in unhealthy loops** — porn, scrolling, gaming, comparison, lust, addiction
- **Disenfranchised** — feels modern culture has nothing for him; left out of the dominant narrative
- **Searching for purpose** — knows there's more than the grind, but doesn't know where to find it
- **Skeptical of religion** — fears it'll be cheesy, judgmental, or fake

## Goals

- Find peace and stillness in a noisy world
- Become a stronger, more disciplined version of himself
- Build real brotherhood with men who push him
- Discover what a man is actually for — purpose, calling, mission
- Get answers to the hard questions about life, suffering, and meaning
- Feel grounded in something ancient and unshakeable

## Where He Hangs Out

- **Instagram, TikTok, YouTube Shorts** — primary discovery channels
- **YouTube long-form** — Jordan Peterson, Joe Rogan, Theo Von, Andrew Huberman, Jocko Willink, Cal Newport, Jon Tyson, Ruslan KD
- **Podcasts** — same names plus comedy and self-improvement
- **Discord, Reddit** — for niche interest communities
- **Gym, gaming, sports**
- **Daily Stoic, Stoic app, men's-self-improvement adjacent content**

## What He Values

- **Authenticity** — can sniff out fake from a mile away
- **Aesthetics** — wears Alo, Lululemon, Stussy, Aimé Leon Dore, Essentials. Premium minimal beats logo-loud.
- **Discipline + earned strength** — respects people who actually do the work
- **Direct truth over comforting lies** — even when it's uncomfortable
- **Community of high-standards men** — doesn't want to be coddled
- **Ancient wisdom** — gravitates to stoicism, philosophy, history; primed for Christianity if presented right

## What Would Turn Him Off Instantly

- Corny worship music videos
- "God's got this!" energy
- Half-smiling, performative joy
- Christian "side hugs" energy
- Pastors trying to sound cool
- Generic motivational quotes overlaid on stock photos
- Anything that talks down to him
- Politicized takes on culture-war hot buttons
- "Just trust the process" toxic positivity
