## Notes for production

- **Seedance iterations:** Expect 2–3 generations per video before it lands. Budget ~$1.05 per post, ~$12 total for the 7 videos with iteration room. Static fallbacks can be generated in DALL-E / Midjourney / Seedance static mode at much lower cost if needed.
- **Image consistency:** Every shot should feel like it's from the same film. Dark, moody, cinematic. Deep jet blacks + warm bone + earth tones + occasional gold rim. Never bright, saturated, or colorful.
- **Caption formatting:** Single-word lines and short sentences with line breaks. Instagram strips double line breaks on mobile — stick to single line breaks between ideas.
- **Your IG bio while these run** (see `production-queue.md` for final locked copy):
  - Name: **FINDGOD**
  - Tagline: **Iron sharpens iron.**
  - Link: `findgod.com`
- **What to track:** saves + shares > likes. Likes are vanity. Saves mean it hit them. Shares mean they want others to see it.
- **Engagement rule:** Reply to every comment and DM in the first 48 hours. Warm, direct, never corny. Signals the algorithm that your posts drive real conversation.
- **Cross-post cadence:** Every post goes to Instagram Reels/Feed + TikTok + YouTube Shorts. Same vertical clip, native upload on each platform (not re-export), same caption, same hashtags.
